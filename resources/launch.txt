There are two ways to launch the project: via Maven and via Intellij IDEA. Second options is preferable because it gives the control of the build like debugging

Maven - mvn clean javafx:run

Intellij IDEA
    1) download javafx-sdk
    2) add to vm options --module-path "C:\Program Files\Java\javafx-21\lib" --add-modules javafx.controls,javafx.media
